Design Work for Bitcamp 2021

Bitcamp is a 36 hour hackathon and the prime opportunity to delve into your curiosities, learn something new, and build something awesome. As part of the design team, I created social media posts and designed Bitcamp's websites to build a positive environment for those passionate in all aspects of technology and promote its mission to all.

Hacking Platform

Feb 2021

The event platform is where hackers will get accounts to track events, form teams, compete with other teams, and get links to specific workshops and sponsor-hosted calls. For this platform, I designed the "Sponsorship Tent," which will list all the sponsors of Bitcamp 2021. Each company will have their own specific page that includes information about their company, job openings, one-on-one meetings for hackers, contact information, and other relevant materials.
